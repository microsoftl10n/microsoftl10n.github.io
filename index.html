Microsoft Cloud + AI International Community Leaderboard

Welcome to Microsoft Cloud + AI International Community Leaderboard! This leaderboard shows a full list of our contributors with points earned from the contributions to Microsoft Localization Community Platform and Microsoft Documentation. You can access the MVP Specific Leaderboard here. 

The Leaderboard explained

How are points calculated?

Content Contribution (Micrososft Docs)

- 15 points for each PR Suggestion

- 60 points for each Approved PR Suggestion

NOTE: Suggestions are reviewed and approved/rejected by linguistic moderators.

Example: a contributor submits a pull request with 4 suggestions. They receive 4 x 15 = 60 points. If the suggestions are approved, then the contributor receives an additional 4 x 60 = 240 points. 

Software Contribution (Microsoft Localization Community Platform)

- Each Translated Word is worth 2 points.

- Each Approved Translated Word is worth 8 points. (*A string of words is approved when it receives 2 or more votes from other contributors on MLCP.)

- Each Vote Given is worth 2 points. (A Vote Given is when a contributor votes for a suggestion that isn't their own)

Example 1: A contributor translates a string with 9 words. They will receive 9 x 2 = 18 points This string is approved when it receives 2 or more votes from the community. The contributor receives an additional 9 x 8 = 72 points.

Example 2: A contributor votes for 5 translated strings, that have all been translated by another contributor. The contributor who has voted gets 5 x 2 = 10 points.

NOTE: we only show the contributors who earn 100 points or more in the leaderboard.


Who are MVPs?

Microsoft Most Valuable Professionals, or MVPs, are technology experts who passionately share their knowledge with the community. They are always on the "bleeding edge" and have an unstoppable urge to get their hands on new, exciting technologies. They have very deep knowledge of Microsoft products and services, while also being able to bring together diverse platforms, products and solutions, to solve real world problems. MVPs make up a global community of over 4,000 technical experts and community leaders across 90 countries and are driven by their passion, community spirit, and quest for knowledge. Above all and in addition to their amazing technical abilities, MVPs are always willing to help others - that's what sets them apart.

You can find more details at the Microsoft MVP site.
